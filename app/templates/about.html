What is PetVaxHK?

PetVaxHK is a local-first pet vaccination tracker designed specifically for Hong Kong pet owners. It helps you:

  • Track vaccination records for your pets
  • Receive timely reminders for upcoming vaccinations
  • Stay compliant with Hong Kong AFCD regulations
  • Find registered vet clinics in Hong Kong

Hong Kong Vaccination Requirements

PetVaxHK helps you comply with AFCD (Agriculture, Fisheries and Conservation Department) regulations:

Dogs

  • Rabies vaccination: Required annually for all dogs over 5 months
  • DHPP: Recommended for protection against distemper, hepatitis, parvo, parainfluenza
  • Dog licence: Must be obtained within 5 months of vaccination

Cats

  • Rabies vaccination: Required for imported cats
  • FVRCP: Recommended for all cats (Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, Panleukopenia)

⚠️ For import requirements, please check the AFCD website for the latest quarantine and vaccination rules based on your country of origin.
